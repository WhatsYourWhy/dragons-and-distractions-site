          <li>Take three breaths, then three moves: sit up, feet down, stand, walk to water, splash face.</li>
        </ul>
      </article>
      <article class="ritual-entry">
        <h3>Rope of Returning</h3>
        <ul>
          <li>Text an ally: "Pull me out in 10?"</li>
          <li>Set a timer, and when it rings, step outside the cave and reply with a quick selfie or voice note.</li>
        </ul>
      </article>
      <article class="ritual-entry">
        <h3>Hibernation with a Clock</h3>
        <ul>
          <li>If you rest, set a 20-90 minute timer.</li>
          <li>Name your wake-up action before lying down, like "When it rings, I open blinds and drink water."</li>
