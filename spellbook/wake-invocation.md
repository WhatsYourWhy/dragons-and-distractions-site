    <section class="field-guide__card">
      <h2>The ritual</h2>
      <ol>
        <li>Place the alarm far enough away that your body has to stand.</li>
        <li>Prepare one command phrase the night before.</li>
        <li>Turn on light, water, and one body cue immediately.</li>
        <li>Send one accountability signal before you sit back down.</li>
        <li>Name the first action of the day before your brain opens side quests.</li>
      </ol>
    </section>

    <section class="field-guide__card">
      <h2>Why it helps</h2>
      <ul>
        <li>It reduces morning decision load.</li>
        <li>It uses body cues instead of waiting for motivation.</li>
        <li>It makes the first movement feel smaller and more scripted.</li>
      </ul>
    </section>

    <section class="field-guide__card field-guide__card--quote">
      <h2>Use it when</h2>
      <div class="field-guide__quote">
        <p>You know what comes next, but your body is still trying to negotiate with the blankets.</p>
        <p>The goal is momentum first, not feeling fully convinced before you move.</p>
      </div>
    </section>
