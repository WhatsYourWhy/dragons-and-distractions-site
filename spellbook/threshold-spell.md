  <div class="field-guide__grid">
    <section class="field-guide__card field-guide__card--quote">
      <h2>The spell</h2>
      <div class="field-guide__quote">
        <p>I do not need to leave the cave entirely. I only need to reach the doorway.</p>
        <p>Three breaths, three moves, then I decide what comes next.</p>
      </div>
    </section>

    <section class="field-guide__card">
      <h2>How to use it</h2>
      <ol>
        <li>Stand in a literal or figurative doorway: the bedroom door, the reply button, the first sentence of the email.</li>
        <li>Take three slow breaths in the threshold.</li>
        <li>Make three small moves: sit up, feet on floor, stand. Or: open app, read one line, type one word.</li>
        <li>From the doorway, decide: one more step, or rest here. Either answer is legal.</li>
      </ol>
    </section>

    <section class="field-guide__card">
      <h2>Why it helps</h2>
      <ul>
        <li>It shrinks the ask from "rejoin the world" to "reach the door."</li>
        <li>It uses body movement to interrupt the freeze loop.</li>
        <li>It gives you a decision point after motion, not before it.</li>
      </ul>
    </section>

    <section class="field-guide__card field-guide__card--quote">
      <h2>Use it when</h2>
      <div class="field-guide__quote">
        <p>Rest has become retreat, and the gap between "I want to" and "I am doing it" keeps widening.</p>
        <p>A text, a doorway step, or a tiny body cue helps more than making a bigger plan.</p>
      </div>
    </section>
