## What helps most

If you are reporting something broken — a dead link, a monster that sends you to the wrong next step, a printable that will not print cleanly, or a page that feels confusing on a low-energy day — the most useful note includes:

- the page URL
- what you expected
- what happened instead
- your device or browser, if it looks like a display issue
